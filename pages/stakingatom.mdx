# Staking ATOM with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake ATOM with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**Keplr**

- Download Keplr from the [Keplr Website](https://www.keplr.app/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

<Steps>

### Go to staking page and connect wallet

Enter the StaFiHub App, and connect your Keplr wallet to StaFiHub and CosmosHub.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_1.png)

### Confirm connected wallets

Once connected, click on “Confirm”

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_2.png)

### Select staking token

Click on “Stake Atom” on the rToken Dashboard.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_3.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the desired amount of ATOM to be staked. Then, provide your StaFi Hub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of rATOM to be received by clicking on the button.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_4.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show the amount of ATOM you are about to stake and its associated gas fees. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of ATOM. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

<Callout type="info">
Do not edit the memo shown on the Keplr transaction. Editing the memo will cause the transaction to fail.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_5.png)

### Wait for confirmations

Your transaction can be tracked through the notification bar. Upon success, your rATOM will appear on the [rATOM Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rATOM/dashboard).

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomhowtostake_6.png)

</Steps>

## The rATOM Token

rATOM: https://www.mintscan.io/stafi/assets

Additionally, rATOM holders can participate in liquidity mining. To learn more about this opportunity, please [visit this page](https://app.stafi.io/rpool?program=lp).

## Commission Fee

10% of ATOM staking rewards will be allocated to StaFi DAO and validator.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [rATOM Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rATOM/stake), and click on “Redeem rATOM” to start unstaking

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of ATOM to be unstaked. Then, provide your StaFiHub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of ATOM to be received by clicking on the “Unbond” button.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomunstake_2.png)

### Confirm details

Confirm the following information on the pop-up page: the amount of unbonded rATOM, Commission Fees (initially set at 0.2%), Relay Fee, Unbonding Period, and the number of ATOM received. Once confirmed, click on "unbond".

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomunstake_3.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show associated gas fees for the transaction. Please review the information carefully. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomunstake_4.png)

### Wait for confirmation

Upon successful unstaking, the amount of unbonding ATOM will be displayed on the rATOM Dashboard under the “unbonded records” section. After the unbonding period ends, tokens will automatically be sent to the receiving address.

![StaFi Staked ATOM](/image/ratomunstake_5.png)

</Steps>
