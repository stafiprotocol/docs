## How to stake KSM with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for **[Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd)** or **[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [KSM staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/KSM?tokenStandard=Native) and click on "Connect Wallet".

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmhowtostake_1.png)

### Connect wallets

Connect StaFi Chain and Polkdot wallet and confirm.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmhowtostake_2.png)

### Click stake and input stake amount

When wallets are connectd, click the "Stake" button and input the staking amount.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmhowtostake_3.png)

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmhowtostake_4.png)

### Confirm the transaction

Enter password to sign the transaction using your Polkadotjs wallet.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmhowtostake_5.png)

### Waiting for completed

Congratulations! Your KSM staking process has been successfully completed. You will now earn rewards for staking your KSM.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rdothowtostake_6.png)

</Steps>

## Commission Fee

10% of KSM staking rewards will be allocated to StaFi DAO and validator.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [KSM unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/DOT?tokenStandard=Native) and click on "Connect Wallet".

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

To unstake KSM, you will need to provide the desired amount of KSM to be unstaked and then click on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmunstake_2.png)

### Confirm the transaction

Enter password to sign the transaction using your Polkadotjs wallet.

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmunstake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

The unstaking operation will be successfully completed. Your tokens will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended (~8days).

![StaFi Staked KSM](/image/rksmunstake_4.png)

</Steps>
