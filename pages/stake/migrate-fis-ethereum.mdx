title: Migrate $FIS to Ethereum in a Few Simple Steps
---

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'
import { Steps } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout type="info">
**Before You Start**

- Double-check that you connected the correct StaFi Chain account before signing.
- Make sure your Ethereum wallet address is the one you want to receive the migrated tokens with. **Prepare a small amount of ETH on that address** (about **0.001 ETH** is enough) to pay gas for the **claim** transaction.
- Only sign the migration request after confirming the wallet addresses and token amount are correct.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Connect your Polkadot wallet

Go to [https://app.stafi.io/migration/fis/](https://app.stafi.io/migration/fis/) and click “Connect Polkadot Wallet”.

![Connect Polkadot Wallet](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-01.png)

<Callout type="warning">
**Note:** Make sure you select the **StaFi Chain account holding the $FIS you want to migrate** in your Polkadot wallet.
</Callout>

![StaFi Chain account selection](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-02.png)

![Polkadot wallet connected](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-03.png)

### Connect your Ethereum wallet

Click “Connect Ethereum Wallet” and connect the Ethereum wallet that will receive your ERC-20 $FIS. That address should already hold a little ETH (about **0.001 ETH** is enough) so you can pay gas when you **claim** on Ethereum.

![Connect Ethereum wallet](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-04.png)

### Confirm and migrate

Once both wallets are connected, the interface will display your StaFi Chain address and the amount of \$FIS available for migration. Click **“Migrate”**, then sign the request in your Polkadot wallet to complete the migration. Your \$FIS will then be migrated to Ethereum.

![Confirm and migrate](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-05.png)

### Add ERC-20 $FIS to MetaMask

Click “Add FIS to MetaMask” to add ERC-20 $FIS to your wallet and view your migrated balance.

Token contract: [0xef3a930e1ffffacd2fc13434ac81bd278b0ecc8d](https://etherscan.io/address/0xef3a930e1ffffacd2fc13434ac81bd278b0ecc8d)

![Add FIS to MetaMask](/image/migration/fis/fis-migration-06.png)

</Steps>
