# Staking BNB with StaFi
import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout type="error">
StaFi has taken the rBNB service offline. No further rewards will be distributed.

Stakers should unstake and withdraw their assets via the BSC Explorer by following these steps:

Step 1: Query your rBNB balance
1. Go to https://bscscan.com/address/0xf027e525d491ef6ffcc478555fbb3cfabb3406a6#readContract
2. Click "Contract" -> "Read Contract"
3. Call `balanceOf` with:
    - account: your account address

Step 2: Approve
1. Go to https://bscscan.com/address/0xf027e525d491ef6ffcc478555fbb3cfabb3406a6#writeContract
2. Click "Contract" -> "Write Contract"
3. Connect wallet
4. Call `increaseAllowance` with:
    - Spender: 0x715e172494bda5636d70fa0714f4e65843cbf9bf
    - Amount: your rBNB balance

Step 3: Unstake and Withdraw 
1. Go to https://bscscan.com/address/0x715e172494bda5636d70fa0714f4e65843cbf9bf#writeProxyContract
2. Click "Contract" -> "Write Proxy"
3. Connect wallet
4. Call the `unstakeAndWithdrawAll` method
5. Your asset will be transferred to your wallet instantly
</Callout>
