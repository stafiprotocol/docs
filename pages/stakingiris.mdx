# Staking IRIS with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake IRIS with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**Keplr**

- Download Keplr from the [Keplr Website](https://www.keplr.app/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

<Steps>

### Go to staking page and connect wallet

Enter the StaFiHub App, and connect your Keplr wallet to StaFiHub and CosmosHub.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_1.png)

### Confirm connected wallets

Once connected, click on “Confirm”

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_2.png)

### Select staking token

Click on “Stake IRIS on the rToken Dashboard.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_3.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the desired amount of IRIS to be staked. Then, provide your StaFi Hub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of rATOM to be received by clicking on the button.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_4.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show the amount of IRIS you are about to stake and its associated gas fees. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of IRIS. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

<Callout type="info">
Do not edit the memo shown on the Keplr transaction. Editing the memo will cause the transaction to fail.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_5.png)

### Wait for confirmations

Your transaction can be tracked through the notification bar. Upon success, your rIRIS will appear on the [rIRIS Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rIRIS/dashboard).

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririshowtostake_6.png)

</Steps>

## The rIRIS Token

rIRIS: https://www.mintscan.io/stafi/assets

Additionally, rIRIS holders can participate in liquidity mining. To learn more about this opportunity, please [visit this page](https://app.stafi.io/rpool?program=lp).

## Commission Fee

10% of IRIS staking rewards will be allocated to StaFi DAO and validator.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [rIRIS Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rIRIS/stake), and click on “Redeem rIRIS” to start unstaking

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririsunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of IRIS to be unstaked. Then, provide your StaFiHub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of ATOM to be received by clicking on the “Unbond” button.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririsunstake_2.png)

### Confirm details

Confirm the following information on the pop-up page: the amount of unbonded rIRIS, Commission Fees (initially set at 0.2%), Relay Fee, Unbonding Period, and the number of ATOM received. Once confirmed, click on "unbond".

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririsunstake_3.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show associated gas fees for the transaction. Please review the information carefully. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririsunstake_4.png)

### Wait for confirmation

Upon successful unstaking, the amount of unbonding IRIS will be displayed on the rATOM Dashboard under the “unbonded records” section. After the unbonding period ends, tokens will automatically be sent to the receiving address.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/ririsunstake_5.png)


</Steps>
